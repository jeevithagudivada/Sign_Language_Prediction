Sign Language



American sign language predictor helps the world to bridge the gap between
signers and non-signers. The application takes in American sign language as
input from signers and translates it into text format for the non-signers so that
they communicate easily with them. The project has been developed by a
group of seven enthusiastic students as a part of software design course under
the guidance of Prof. Lidia Morrison at California State University Fullerton Let's Go Ahead and Explore :)
